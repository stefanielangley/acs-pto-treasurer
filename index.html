A ACS AZLE CHRISTIAN SCHOOL PTO Treasurer Kickoff Meeting agenda & new treasurer checklist Print / Save as PDF Reset checklist Purpose Align on the PTO's current financial position, clarify responsibilities and controls, and confirm priorities for the year so I can step into the treasurer role smoothly. Meeting Agenda 1. Welcome & role transition • Quick introductions, if needed • Confirm treasurer responsibilities and expectations • Identify who I should partner with most closely during the transition 2. Current financial snapshot • Current bank balance(s) • Cash on hand • Outstanding checks, bills, reimbursements, or unpaid commitments • Any restricted or earmarked funds 3. Budget status • Approved annual budget • Actual income and expenses year to date • Major categories running over or under plan • Upcoming expenses we already know are coming 4. Fundraising plan & event economics • Major fundraisers on the calendar • Expected revenue and costs by event • Lessons learned from last year's fundraising results • Biggest financial risks or uncertainties 5. Spending approvals & controls • Who can approve spending and up to what amount • Reimbursement process and turnaround expectations • Receipt and documentation requirements • Process for checks, debit cards, online payments, and cash handling 6. Banking, systems & access • Bank account signers • Online banking access • Accounting spreadsheet/software access • Location of historical records, reports, and supporting documents 7. Compliance & reporting cadence • Monthly treasurer report expectations • Tax filings, nonprofit filings, and deadlines • Audit or financial review expectations • Insurance or bonding, if applicable 8. Open questions & next steps • Biggest issues from last year • Immediate action items for the next 30 days • Any decisions the board needs to make soon Decisions to Leave With • Who owns what in the financial process • What reporting format and cadence the board wants • What controls are in place today and what needs to be tightened up • Top 3 financial priorities for this semester/year New Treasurer Checklist Tap items to check them off — your progress is saved on this device.
