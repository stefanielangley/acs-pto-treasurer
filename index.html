PTO Treasurer Kickoff

Meeting agenda & new treasurer checklist

Purpose

Align on the PTO's current financial position, clarify responsibilities and controls, and confirm priorities for the year so I can step into the treasurer role smoothly.

Meeting Agenda

1. Welcome & role transition

5 minutes
  • Quick introductions, if needed
  • Confirm treasurer responsibilities and expectations
  • Identify who I should partner with most closely during the transition

2. Current financial snapshot

10 minutes
  • Current bank balance(s)
  • Cash on hand
  • Outstanding checks, bills, reimbursements, or unpaid commitments
  • Any restricted or earmarked funds

3. Budget status

10 minutes
  • Approved annual budget
  • Actual income and expenses year to date
  • Major categories running over or under plan
  • Upcoming expenses we already know are coming

4. Fundraising plan & event economics

10 minutes
  • Major fundraisers on the calendar
  • Expected revenue and costs by event
  • Lessons learned from last year's fundraising results
  • Biggest financial risks or uncertainties

5. Spending approvals & controls

10 minutes
  • Who can approve spending and up to what amount
  • Reimbursement process and turnaround expectations
  • Receipt and documentation requirements
  • Process for checks, debit cards, online payments, and cash handling

6. Banking, systems & access

5 minutes
  • Bank account signers
  • Online banking access
  • Accounting spreadsheet/software access
  • Location of historical records, reports, and supporting documents

7. Compliance & reporting cadence

5 minutes
  • Monthly treasurer report expectations
  • Tax filings, nonprofit filings, and deadlines
  • Audit or financial review expectations
  • Insurance or bonding, if applicable

8. Open questions & next steps

5 minutes
  • Biggest issues from last year
  • Immediate action items for the next 30 days
  • Any decisions the board needs to make soon

Decisions to Leave With

  • Who owns what in the financial process
  • What reporting format and cadence the board wants
  • What controls are in place today and what needs to be tightened up
  • Top 3 financial priorities for this semester/year

Simple Opening Script

"Hello, y'all — I'd love to use today to get aligned on our current financial position, understand how the board wants reporting to work, and make sure I have what I need to support the PTO well. My goal is to leave with a clear picture of where we stand, what is coming up, and any priorities I need to jump on right away."
